A blog, again

What was wrong with the old blog?

  1. I had trouble getting the formatting just right.
  2. Google does a nice job of tracking your users and pageviews, but I don't want to know those things.
  3. I'd like to move this to my own webserver, eventually.

Why blog?

If you're not suspicious about your impulses to tell the world what you're thinking, maybe you should be. Sometimes I consider heading down to Kinko's, printing out twenty copies of some handwritten newsletter, and sending it off to those I think might care. On the other hand, there's that possibility of a kindred spirit stumbling across your writing.
